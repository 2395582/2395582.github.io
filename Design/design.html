DESIGN

Design documents

As can be seen in my wireframe, I wanted this website to err on the side of caution when it came to simplicity.

Instead of cluttering it with anything that could be deemed unnecessary. I did add the 'filmstrip' both to add some visual appeal in keeping with the theme, and to stop my website from looking like a word document.

I was originally going to go with a black and white color scheme, but found it lacked the visual appeal I wanted.

I left any decoration out of the blog pages, to ensure ease of reading without any distractions.
