Popped Corn & Dollar Bills

The movie industry is a multi-billion dollar dream machine. Decades of work projected on to the silver screen to entertain audiences... and make money.

While the notion that art is not made with the intention of filling bank accounts is a very romantic one, it is undeniable that the lining of pockets is an important by-product of this industry.

And while, I'll be the first to admit that a large box office does not always indicate a good film, the money does tend to come into play.

So join me, and take a look at the highest earning movies of each decade, starting from the roaring Twenties and ending with the spandex clad summer blockbusters of today.

About

The data provided will display the three highest grossing movies of each decade, in the past century. Naturally, due to inflation, differences will be blown a bit out of proportion, but some information can be garnered from these graphs.

Information such as the “genre shift” humanity has taken over the past 100 years. Where cowboys and gunslingers once reigned supreme, now stand men in tights. Cultural fixations reflected in movies over the decades will become apparent with the information of which movies were most popular with which audiences.

The data on the stories we loved, will tell us stories of their own.
