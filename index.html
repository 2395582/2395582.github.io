Popped Corn & Critics

Every decade brings something change to the movie industry. Be it fancy effects, or the very tone inherent in that decade. The fact remains, every time period has something to offer, although, some more than others.

Which decade has the best movies? Is it the 70's with it's gritty portrayal of realities? The 40's with the dark, distinct film noir? The 2010's with it's spandex clad, caped crusades?

The Data provided, will, hopefully answer this question.

About

The data provided will display the top 250 highest ranking movies, as per the famous list on IMDb.

By analyzing the data, we will be able to see which decade had the most "best" movies. Is dad right when he says, "They don't make 'em like that anymore"?

The data on the stories we loved, will tell us stories of their own.
